The Healthy Mind Platter

Do you ever feel like you have too little time? Are you constantly behind on tasks? Or cannot focus on that thing that you have to do?

This website follows the Healthy Mind Platter, a model that highlights seven "mental nutrients" that you need throughout the day to optimise brain capacity.

Use this site for you or your team to make the most of your brain capacity, stop procrastinating and learn!

Click on one of the pieces in the platter to access tips & tricks on how to keep your mind healthy and balanced.

You only have one brain - take care of it! ♥
