Organize
your desk

If your desk is a mess, your mind will also be. And that makes it harder to focus. Just imagine – you’re trying to get your mind to work on the task at hand, but how can you zero in on that when your working space is cluttered and unorganized?

Take a moment to tidy up the space where you work. Organize the stacks of notes, books and pens so that you have an overview of everything you're working with and notice how your focus is much sharper.

Now you’ve accomplished one thing already and have one less thing on your mind. With a neat desk you can now focus much easier.
