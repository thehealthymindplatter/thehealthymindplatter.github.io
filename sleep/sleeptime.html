Sleep Time

Getting a good night's sleep is crucial for you to better remember what you have learnt during the day. It also boosts your creativity, attentive abilities and problem solving skills. Getting enough sleep helps you to deal with emotions and to make better decisions. On top of that - a healthy sleep schedule strengthens your immune system and metabolism.
