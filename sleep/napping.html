Tip 4 napping

Sleeping does not only have to happen during the nights. Do you want to take a productivity boosting nap but are not sure how to? Try out this exercise for progressive muscle relaxation which will help you relax your body and eventually fall asleep.

  1. Lay down and relax your body
  2. As you breath in - tense a muscle group, and as you breath out - relax it again
  3. Go through your whole body until you feel completely relaxed
  4. Clench your hands, wrinkle your forehead and squeeze your butt together tightly
  5. Try it out for yourself. Happy napping!
