Take a walk

Sometimes just to get your body moving is all you need. If you don't have enough time to work out you could still be active by talking a walk.

Get your shoes on and head outside for at least 30 minutes. Allow your body to do what it was designed to do through thousands of years of evolution - move around. It will raise your heart rate and circulate more oxygen through the blood.

Stroll around and enjoy your neighbourhood, go to an area you haven't been before, use your feet!
