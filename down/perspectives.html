Down time means to let go of all the things you must do and everything that stresses you. Just picking up your phone when you are on a break does not relax your mind and will not help you focus better. Try to limit the amount of impressions you recieve during your break.

Taking a walk in nature has tremendous effects when it comes to lowering stress levels. For your next break - try to go outside. As a challenge - leave your phone and headphones at home. Do not let yourself be distracted by music in your ears or notifications in your pocket.

If you have access to nature - that's great. Otherwise, try to just take a walk around your neighborhood. Let your mind wander and come back with new perspectives and energy.
