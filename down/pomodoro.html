What is the Pomodoro technique? It's not a recipe for the perfect pasta sauce, but rather an effective recipe for optimising your productivity.

The Pomodoro technique was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980's and is widely used across the world for effective time management. With this technique, you will optimise your focus by making sure that you are including regular breaks into your work day.

So how do you do it?

  1. Pick a task
  2. Set a 25-minute timer
  3. Work on your task until the time is up
  4. Take a 5 minute break
  5. Every 4 pomodoros, take a longer 15-30 minute break
